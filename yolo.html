About Me

Master candidate in Electrical and Computers Engineering at FEUP - University of Porto.

My research interests are related to the concept and development of low-cost industrial robots: development of robotic manipulators and software implementation of mobile robotic solutions. I am also interested in multi-robot communications and sensor data identification/estimation.

In my dissertation research, I will focus on design, modeling and implementation of a hyper-redundant robotic manipulator.

I'm a member of NuIEEE, a student branch of IEEE in my University.

I am also a member of UT, a football team founded by me and some friends in 2010.
